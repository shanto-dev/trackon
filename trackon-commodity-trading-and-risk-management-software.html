International trade is the essential roadmap that guides the global economy. Now, the role of businesses limited to local markets is shrinking in trade. Expanding into foreign markets requires several operational processes to be carried out in coordination for companies.

It is now possible for even small companies to carry out activities such as production, purchasing, transportation, marketing, accounting, sales in different countries quickly, effectively, with minimum costs, and profitably. We are talking about software that will allow you to manage all these processes with a small team.

Trackon is the commodity international trading and risk management software. It serves a wide range of companies of different sizes, from export-import-oriented traders to multinational companies with hundreds of employees. The software targets the flow dynamics of business processes and the integration of growing organizations into a seamless supply chain.

Trackon Software’s flagship product is her Agro Commodities Trading software. Built by traders for trading, this software offers excellent functionality, runs on the web, and has impressive features.

How It Works

Trackon software has flexible approval flows for each department, and it is software that can be aligned with each company’s existing workflow processes. A demo may be requested to see how it works and to test its compatibility with your current workflow.

The software acts as a bridge between different departments and different workstations to ensure continuity in workflow processes. It provides the elimination of many disruptions, including document transfer, re-document preparation, data loss during information transfer, erroneous transactions, and operational delays between departments.

Let’s take a company that carries out trading activities around the world. It will be necessary to prepare single files, proforma, and invoices in different currencies such as EUR, USD, RMB. You will need to work simultaneously and integrate between offices or units in different countries, including accounting and finance processes. Based on the contracts, order forms, or approved invoices, production, customs clearance, and logistics processes will have to be carried out in harmony. Imagine that all these processes take place with software adapted to your company, with zero errors and the ability to take immediate action.

Trackon software offers features such as multi-Currency, multi-Branch, multi-Language, multi-Step Execution for your company. Moreover, you do not need to invest in an extra IT workforce to backup or manage the database by working on a cloud basis.

Trading Software: Non-personal business processes

Due to the nature of the trade, can some disruptions and mistakes be experienced? Many companies use this excuse, ignoring personal employee faults or organizational glitches. Anyway, Trackon assists the execution team in avoiding costly expenses with its built-in triggers, checklists, and steps flow design to move the supply chain seamlessly.

Many companies experience disruptions in their workflows due to employees being unable to stay at work due to illness, vacation, or various excuses. The term key personnel comes from the fact that a company’s workflow processes are dependent on people. For these reasons, your workflows are complete.

For these reasons, all organizational activities of your company should be processed through a system, and everything should be recorded. You should be able to view and report every stage. No longer be dependent on an individual employee!
