FAQs on Trackon Commodity Trade and Risk Management Software

What is a CTRM solution?

CTRM or Commodity Trading and Risk Management software is developed and used for trading companies that buy and sell commodities and need to track and manage their operations from start to end. With CTRM software you can monitor and manage steps such as accounting, shipping, logistics, delivery and the risk management involved in all that.

Does Trackon include accounting software for logistics companies?

Yes, Trackon commodity trade and risk management software, can also be used as accounting software for logistics companies.

Can Trackon CTRM be used as ERP software?

Yes, depending on your industry need, you can also use Trackon CTRM software as ERP.

Who can use Trackon CTRM commodity trading risk management?

Trackon CTRM commodity trading risk management is the perfect software for soft commodities including dairy, meat and poultry, grains, etc.
