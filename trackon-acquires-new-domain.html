Trackon Acquires New Domain

  • July 31, 2024
    • -
  • by sozturk

Exciting News from Trackon!

We are thrilled to announce that Trackon has acquired the domain name trackon.com As part of this strategic move, we have successfully transitioned our website from trackonsoftware.com to the new and improved address at trackon.com.

This change marks a significant milestone in our journey, reflecting our commitment to enhancing your user experience and making it easier for you to access our cutting-edge CTRM software solutions. The new domain is more concise and memorable, aligning with our goal to streamline your interactions with our brand.

What This Means for You:

  • Seamless Transition: All traffic from trackonsoftware.com will be automatically redirected to trackon.com, ensuring a smooth transition without any disruption to your experience.
  • Enhanced User Experience: Our new domain is designed to be more user-friendly, making it easier for you to find the information and services you need.
  • Continued Excellence: Rest assured, our commitment to providing top-notch CTRM software solutions remains unchanged. We will continue to innovate and deliver the best products and services to support your trading operations.

We invite you to explore our new website at trackon.com and stay updated with the latest news and developments from Trackon.

Thank you for your continued support as we embark on this exciting new chapter!
